Polin

In June 2014 we won the first place in a competition and were selected to design the Core Exhibition in the Museum of the History of Polish Jews. As a result, we cooperated with the Museum for several months on the Polin. 1000 Year History of Polish Jews publication, where we were responsible for the visual design and typesetting.

We prepared a catalogue in English and in Polish (235x297mm, 432 pages).
Scope
Graphic design, Typesetting
Input
We received the textual content and images (documents and gallery photos) from the authors of the Core Exhibition in the Museum.
Process
Our main responsibility was to design a layout with coherent textual and visual layers, where both layers guide the readers through the 1,000 years of history (divided into 8 galleries).
Output
We prepared a catalogue in English and in Polish (235x297mm, 432 pages). See more here: http://www.polin.pl/pl/aktualnosci/2014/11/20/katalog-wystawy-stalej-polin-1000-lat-historii-zydo w-polskich
