Big A

BigA (Duże A) is a year-round project, a part of the Wrocław Division of the Association of Polish Architects (SARP Wrocław) activity, which consists of lectures and workshops delivered by established architects and urban planners. We created visual designs and typesetting for the special edition of the BigA project as part of the European Capital of Culture Wrocław 2016 agenda.
Scope
Graphic design, Typesetting
Input
We received textual content and illustrations from the Association of Polish Architects (the organiser and publisher of the project).
Process
We designed the layout of the catalogue and the elements of BigA’s visual identity: the logo, the graphic, the colour palette and typography.
Output
We created the catalogue in Polish and English (162 pages, 210x210mm). Learn more about the BigA project here: http://www.wroclaw.sarp.org.pl/pl/duze_a/
