WMA

Wrocław’s Masters of Architecture is a series presenting the oeuvre of the most outstanding architects. Six parts of the series have been published so far, showcasing the work of the designers of the buildings like Kredka and Ołówek, the Auditorium of the Faculty of Chemistry, Solpol, Dolmed, the Africarium or the restaurated Renoma.
Scope
Graphic design, Typesetting
Input
We received the copy and the illustrations from the publisher (the Association of Polish Architects).
Process
We created the layout for the whole series as well as separate designs for each cover.
Output
Our work resulted in six Polish/English publications (82 pages, 148x210mm).
