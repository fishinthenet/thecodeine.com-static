imapp

imapp is a consulting company and a software developer working for the largest institutions and companies from all over the world. imapp is an incredible think tank, analysing the most complex models. The company specialises in enterprise and R&D support, including financial instruments, policy indicators, regional development and cross-border cooperation, energy efficiency and renewable energy sources.

The Codeine was responsible for designing a new visual identity for imapp.
Scope
Branding, Print, Web design, Web development
Input
The client wanted the design to combine subtlety and elegance with a pinch of technology.
Process
The challenge was to find the right colours and to make the contrasting typefaces work together.
Output
The result was a bit eclectic yet elegant and confidence-inspiring visual identity.
