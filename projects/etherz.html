Etherz

Etherz is the first outcome of fiddling with 3D visualizations of a blockchain network. It was inspiring and super fun to work with!

At this stage, Etherz is mainly a toy, which, however, has some educational value as well. Hopefully, it will help popularize blockchain and the Ethereum project, too.

A careful observer will notice that the layout of the assets of the Etherz wallets draws on the Ethereum symbol.
Scope
Idea, Naming, Branding, Web design, Web development
Input
Open and readable data stored on blockchain. Thanks to Etherscan and Ethplorer APIs!
Process
All data is first normalised and then it's generating and positioning the elements (assets) of the address, such way that it reminds a bit Ethereum logo (two flipped cones) and the ETH symbol Îž
Output
3D visualisation of the Ethereum address :) It doesn't need to be boring and flat anymore!
