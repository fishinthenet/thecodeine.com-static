DoFA '15, '16

Lower Silesian Festival of Architecture (Dolnośląski Festiwal Architektury) is a annual project, a part of the Wrocław Division of the Association of Polish Architects (SARP Wrocław). We created visual identities for all 6 editions of the festival, including festival catalog.

DoFA 2015 Catalog – City flows, 160 pages
DoFA 2016 Catalog – Spaces for beauty, 144 pages
Scope
Graphic design, Typesetting
Input
We received textual content and illustrations from the Association of Polish Architects (the organiser and publisher of the project).
Process
We created covers and layouts for the catalogs as well as the elements of DoFA’s visual identity: the logo, the graphical design, the colors and the typography.
Output
Each catalog (210x210mm) has a cardboard cover wh corresponds with the theme of its respective festival edition. More about the project: http://www.wroclaw.sarp.org.pl/pl/dofa/
