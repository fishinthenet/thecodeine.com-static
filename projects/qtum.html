Qtum

Qtum (a short for Quantum) is a China-based, technologically advanced project combining the best bits of the Bitcoin and Ethereum technologies. It can be considered a space and a universe for a Blockchain business.

At the beginning of 2017 we were asked to create a website for Quantum (the creators of Qtum) and to refresh their logo. Like Golem, Qtum (which already used the visual identity designed by us) raised the funds via ICO and beat the record by gaining 28 million dollars in just a couple of hours.
Scope
Branding, Web design, Web development
Input
The project was incredibly technically complex and there was no identifiable product or service. Apart from the Quantum logo and colours, we had full creative freedom.
Process
A plethora of options and solutions offered by the Qtum technology inspired us to build the Qtum Universe. We created slowly animated geometric shapes resembling the movement of the galaxies and matched them with stylish typography.
Output
Subtle redesign of the logo and the new typography emphasised the technological profile of the company. The whole process was crowned with launching the minimalist website presenting the Qtum universe.
